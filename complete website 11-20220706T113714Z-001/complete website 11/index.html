Welcome to Ispahani Public School and College

With a dream to building educated and enlightened citizens as well as charismatic future leaders for a new born nation, Ispahani Public School was established in the lovely lap of nature in 1979 under the devoted and generous patronisation of distinguished industrialist Late Mirza Ahmed Ispahani. In 1981 the school started functioning by enrolling students in KG-1 to Class-VIII housed in a three-storied building. With the passage of time, the infrastructure has been expanded to another two four and five-storied buildings accommodating the Secondary and Higher Secondary sections, science labs, teachers’ and students’ common room.
